The Human Story Behind the Music
👂 Understanding the gap — Surgery is funded. Lifelong care is not. That's where your partnership makes the difference.
💔

The Silent Crisis

Government programs support cochlear implant surgeries — but the long-term cost of device maintenance overwhelms most families. Without upkeep, children risk losing their restored hearing.

🔧

What Support Covers

Processor replacements, battery packs, mapping services, and audiology follow-ups. Each child has a different need — identified and documented by NISH Trivandrum.

🌱

Measurable Impact

Every rupee of CSR funding is traceable to a specific child and a specific device. Post-event impact reports are provided to all CSR partners for compliance documentation.

India has one of the world's largest populations of children with hearing impairment — and 1 million require cochlear implant surgery. Kerala's government-funded programme gave hundreds of children a second chance at sound. Now, as devices age past their lifespan with no upgrade support, that chance is silently being taken away.
